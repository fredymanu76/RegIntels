# Business Plan

We are a fintech company that provides payment services. This is our business model.

## What We Do

We help people send and receive money. Our platform is easy to use and available online. We target a broad market.

## Revenue

We plan to generate revenue through fees. We have some funding and investment to get started.

## Regulations

We will follow all required regulations and apply for the necessary licences. We plan to seek FCA authorisation.

## Team

We have a small team that will grow as the business expands.

## Future

We expect to grow quickly and become profitable within a few years. Our financial projections look positive.
