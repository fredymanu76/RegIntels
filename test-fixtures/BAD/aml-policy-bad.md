# AML Policy

We take money laundering seriously and have anti-money laundering measures. Our company follows all applicable laws and AML regulations.

## Overview

All staff should be aware of money laundering risks. If you see something suspicious, tell your manager. We are aware of customer due diligence requirements.

## Checks

We do checks on customers when they sign up. We look at their ID and make sure everything seems okay.

## Reporting

If there's a problem, we'll report it to the relevant authorities as needed.

## Training

Staff will receive training as appropriate.

## Review

This policy will be reviewed periodically.
